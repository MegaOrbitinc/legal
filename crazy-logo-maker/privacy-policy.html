Privacy Policy

Crazy Logo Maker — Last updated: April 20, 2026

Crazy Logo Maker ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Crazy Logo Maker application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account or sign in.
  • Usage Data: We track the number of logos generated and exported to enforce free-tier limits (3 generations/day, 5 exports/month).
  • Subscription Data: If you subscribe to Premium, payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not store your full payment card details.
  • AI Prompts: Text prompts you submit are sent to our AI provider (Google Gemini) to generate logos. Prompts are not stored long-term on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's functionality
  • To enforce usage limits and subscription status
  • To communicate about your account or subscription
  • To analyze aggregate usage trends for product improvement

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Google Gemini API: To process your logo generation prompts
  • Google Play Billing: To manage Premium subscriptions
  • RevenueCat: To manage subscription status and entitlements across platforms
  • Analytics providers: Aggregate, non-personally-identifiable usage data

Data Storage & Security

Account and usage data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password.

Your Logo Content

Logos you create belong to you. We do not claim ownership of generated content. Free-tier exports include a watermark; Premium exports are watermark-free with a commercial license.

Children's Privacy

Crazy Logo Maker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@crazylogomaker.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
