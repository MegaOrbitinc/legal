Lightspeed G1 — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

Lightspeed G1 is an Ontario G1 driver's test preparation app developed by Mega Orbit. We believe your study data is yours. This policy explains what information we collect and how we handle it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Lightspeed G1 stores your study progress, quiz results, streaks, badges, and preferences locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

No Accounts

Lightspeed G1 does not require you to create an account. There is no sign-up, login, or cloud sync. All progress is stored on your device using local storage.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking SDKs, or crash reporting tools that send data to third parties.

No Advertising

Lightspeed G1 does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect data, we do not share data with any third parties.

Premium Features

When premium features become available, payment processing will be handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) through their standard in-app purchase systems. We do not see or store your payment card information. Purchase receipts are managed by the platform's billing system.

Children's Privacy

Lightspeed G1 is an educational app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data. We have no access to your data and cannot recover it.

Your Rights

Since no data leaves your device, you have full control over your information at all times. You can clear the app's data at any time through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app's features change. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
