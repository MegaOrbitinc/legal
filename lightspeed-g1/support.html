Common Questions

My progress disappeared

Lightspeed G1 stores all progress locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your progress will be lost. We recommend completing your study before making any changes to the app.

What's included for free?

The free version includes 140+ practice questions, 25 road sign flashcards, mock tests, and 9 POV drive scenarios — everything you need to pass the G1 test.

What is Premium?

Premium features are marked as "coming soon" and include additional practice questions, advanced road sign packs, and extra drive scenarios. Premium is not available for purchase yet.

Is this official?

Lightspeed G1 is an independent study tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Ontario Ministry of Transportation, DriveTest, or any government agency. Question content is based on publicly available Ontario driver's handbook material.
